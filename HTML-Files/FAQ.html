What is web hosting?

Website hosting, or what is known as [Web Hosting], is the place where your website files are stored on the Internet. Hosting consists of a computer [server with very high capabilities in a place called data center or data centers, and it is connected to high speeds from the Internet and works around the clock without stopping..

What is managed hosting?

Managed hosting means that the hosting company takes care of managing all servers, devices, and programs and exempts the customer from that, which gives the customer the opportunity to focus on his site better than wasting time managing his server. This type of hosting is very suitable for small, medium, and sometimes large sites. You can learn about The best companies that provide website hosting Managed joint.

What are the types of web hosting?

There are many different types of hosting, each of which serves its purpose, such as: shared hosting, cloud hosting, WordPress hosting, VPS virtual servers, full servers, and also reseller hosting and many different hosts such as email hosting, Database hosting, and more..
